# Physical * Establish a consistent circadian rhythm to optimize physical and mental well-being. * Avoid consuming cold water and processed sugar to minimize negative impacts on health. * Prioritize grounding physical health as the foundation for overall wellness. # Mental * Adopt the dichotomy of control: focus on what can be controlled and let go of what cannot. * Utilize Socratic questioning to break down delusions and foster critical thinking. * Emphasize action over overthinking to maintain a healthy balance between reflection and progress. # Spiritual * Practice ruthless honesty with oneself to ensure authenticity and integrity. * Maintain a process-oriented mindset rather than focusing solely on outcomes, allowing for greater flexibility and resilience.
